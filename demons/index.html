DEMON SLAYER: KIMETSU NO YAIBA




Demon Slayer, also known as Kimetsu no Yaiba in Japanese, is a popular manga and anime series created by Koyoharu Gotouge. The story follows a young boy named Tanjiro Kamado who becomes a demon slayer after his family is brutally murdered by demons, with his younger sister Nezuko being turned into a demon herself. Tanjiro sets out on a quest to avenge his family and find a cure for his sister's condition, all while battling powerful demons and training to become a skilled swordsman. The series has gained a large following for its compelling characters, emotional storytelling, and stunning animation.
